Hi! Welcome to my page!

I’m Kien, a research economist at the University of Birmingham. My work spans financial economics, climate/environmental economics, and health economics, with interests in macro-financial stability and policy evaluation.

Methodologically, I work with causal inference (DiD/event study/SDID), econometrics, and machine learning, including text-as-data applications for policy and risk analysis.
